This guide walks you through installing the 909Dimensions vertical stand for the Lian Li Dan A4-H2O case. The vertical stand elevates your PC and gives it a compact footprint.

> A downloadable PDF version of the official installation guide is available [here](/static/documents/909Dimensions_LianLiDanA4H2OVerticalStand_InstallationGuide.pdf).

## Before You Begin

### Parts Included

- Vertical stand base
- Hardware kit (4x M3 screws)

### Tools Required

- Allen key (2.5mm)
- Phillips head screwdriver (Optional)
- Clean, flat work surface

### Preparation

1. Power down your PC completely and unplug all cables.
2. Clear a workspace with enough room to lay the case on its front.
3. Handle with care.

## Installation Steps

### Step 0: Position the case

Lay the case flat on the front panel, giving you unrestricted access to the rear IO section.

### Step 1: Remove original thumbscrews if installed

If you don't have the original thumbscrews installed, you can skip this step.

<PostImage src="/static/images/lian-li-dan-a4-h2o-vertical-stand/1.png" alt="Removing the original thumbscrews" subtitle="Removing the original thumbscrews" />

### Step 2: Align the vertical stand with the holes for the thumbscrews

**Rear exit stand:** Ensure the arch opening points towards the back/bottom of the case so you can route cables out the back.

**Side exit stand:** Use the reference slot pictured below to align the reference line with the top of the case; the exit will then be on the correct side for the stand you have.

<PostImage src="/static/images/lian-li-dan-a4-h2o-vertical-stand/ReferenceSlotImage.jpg" alt="Reference slot aligned with top of case" subtitle="Reference slot aligned with top of case" />

Now, your stand should be aligned perfectly. Please note, if you do not align it correctly, one edge may look like it is sticking out further than the other.

<PostImage src="/static/images/lian-li-dan-a4-h2o-vertical-stand/2.png" alt="Aligning the vertical stand with the holes for the thumbscrews" subtitle="Aligning the vertical stand with the holes for the thumbscrews" />

### Step 3: Use the provided screws to secure the vertical stand to the case

These will screw directly into the same holes as the original thumbscrew holes.
 
<span style={{ color: 'darkred', fontWeight: 'bold' }}>Do not overtighten the screws. Overtightening can damage both the vertical stand and the threaded screw holes in the case.</span>

<PostImage src="/static/images/lian-li-dan-a4-h2o-vertical-stand/3.png" alt="Using the provided screws to secure the vertical stand to the case" subtitle="Using the provided screws to secure the vertical stand to the case" />

### Step 4 (Optional): Use philips head screwdriver to remove case feet.

<PostImage src="/static/images/lian-li-dan-a4-h2o-vertical-stand/4.png" alt="Using a philips head screwdriver to remove case feet" subtitle="Using a philips head screwdriver to remove case feet" />

### Step 5 (Not Optional): Enjoy your new vertical stand!

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible solution |
|-------|-------------------|
| Stand feels wobbly | Check that all screws are tight and the base is on a flat surface. |
| Case doesn't align with bracket | Verify you have the correct orientation for the vertical stand. |
| Screws won't thread | Some force may be required to thread the screws into the case, but do not overtighten. |
